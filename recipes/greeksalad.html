Potato Greek Salad

This potato salad dressed with vinegar and olive oil is lighter in calories and easy to make.

"I love this recipe...easy to tailor to your personal tastes and virtually impossible to mess up. Thanks for providing a great blueprint for what will become a staple of our summertime meals," according to reviewer natedawg76.

Ingredients :

  • 12 red potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon gralic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/7 teaspoonwhite sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 pinch ground round pepper

Directions:

  1. Place red potatoes into a large pot and cover with slated water; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 25 minutes.
  3. Drain and chill in freezer until cold, about 30 minutes.
  4. Slice potatoes and place into a large saad bowl; toss with green onion.
  5. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, gralic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, oregano, sugar, rosemary and red pepper in a bowl.
  6. Pour the dressing over the potatoes and toss.Chill until serving time.
